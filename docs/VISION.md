# Vision for Métis

Métis was created to provide organizations with a stable operational platform
that replaces fragmented software ecosystems with a unified infrastructure.

Modern organizations often rely on disconnected systems for communications,
documents, governance, and financial tracking. This fragmentation creates
complexity, operational friction, and reduced institutional clarity.

Métis addresses this by providing a single coordinated system where operational
processes, governance workflows, and organizational data coexist within a
shared architecture.

## Core Philosophy

Métis is built on several principles:

- clarity over complexity
- modular architecture over monolithic systems
- long‑term maintainability over rapid short‑term development
- operational transparency

## Role of Hermes

Hermes represents the intelligence layer of the platform.

While Métis provides infrastructure and governance tools,
Hermes provides system awareness, automation, and analytical support.

Hermes enhances the platform by assisting administrators with tasks,
interpreting system data, and automating operational workflows.

## Long Term Direction

The long-term vision for Métis includes:

- deeper Hermes automation capabilities
- expanded integrations with external services
- plugin ecosystems for modules
- enhanced reporting and analytics

Métis aims to become a durable operational platform capable of evolving
with organizations as their needs grow.
